about

Hello. I am a linux and open-source software enthusiast living in Cape Town, South Africa.

I currently work for the University of Cape Town (UCT) as the Unix Systems Administrator in the Department of Computer Science. I run a large fleet of linux servers and workstations.

Before joining Computer Science I worked for Information and Communication Technology Services (ICTS) as a Network Infrastructure Technical Specialist managing unix servers and predominantly Cisco switches and routers.

I am a founding member of the Western Cape Linux Users Group (CLUG). I previously served as the Secretary for a number of years. I am also a co-founder and a core member of the University of Cape Town Linux Enthusiasts Group (LEG).

My technical interests are predominantly in the areas of unix (especially linux), open-source software, open content, computer programming (especially in python) and task automation. In my spare time I hike, swim, garden, read, play around with electronics and wood work.
